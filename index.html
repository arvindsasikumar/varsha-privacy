Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 May 2026

Summary

Varsha is private by default. The app does not collect, transmit, store on our servers, or share any personal information about you. There are no accounts, no logins, and no analytics.

Data Stored on Your Device

Varsha stores your water intake history, your daily goal, your unit preference (ml or oz), and your reminder settings locally on your device using your device's standard application storage. This data never leaves your device through Varsha.

Uninstalling the app removes all of this data.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Varsha uses your device's standard local notifications to remind you to drink water at the times you configure. Notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No notification content is sent to or routed through any external server.

Network Access

Varsha does not make any network requests. The app does not contact any servers operated by us or by third parties during normal use.

Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

Varsha contains no analytics SDKs, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party identifiers.

Children's Privacy

Varsha does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new date above.
